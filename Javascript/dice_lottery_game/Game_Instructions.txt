Instructions to play the game:
1. Choose any number from 1 to 5 from the number box.
2. Click the button named 'Rool Dice' to roll the dice.
3. If your chosen number matches with the dice, you will win.
4. Acoording to the number, the gift item will be shown below the result.
5. Else if your chosen number doesn't match, you will lose the game.
6. Repeat the same steps to play again and win the prizes.
